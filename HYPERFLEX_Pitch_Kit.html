Objection handling

What they'll say. What you say back.

"Isn't this gambling? I don't want legal issues."
Play-money only — no real money ever changes hands. It's closer to a trivia leaderboard than a sportsbook. No licenses needed, no compliance risk. Think of it like your community's own internal points game.
"I don't have time to manage this."
The AI does it. Paste your YouTube URL and it generates ready-to-publish markets from your comments in 30 seconds. You approve and publish. Takes 2 minutes a week, max.
"My community isn't big enough."
This is actually better with a tighter community. 500 highly engaged members predicting and competing on a leaderboard is more fun than 50,000 passive viewers. Start free, scale up.
"What's the cost?"
Free to start — no credit card. Pro is $29/month when you want unlimited markets and the AI scanner. Most creators launch free and upgrade when they see their audience is hooked.
"How is this different from a poll?"
Polls are static. HYPERFLEX has live moving odds — prices shift as more people bet, just like real prediction markets. Members have skin in the game (their points), a live leaderboard to climb, streak multipliers, and rewards. Engagement is 10x a poll.
"I'd have to get my community to sign up for another thing."
One link, Google sign-in, done. No app to download. You share the link, they click, they're in. It lives at hyperflex.network/[yourname] — as branded as you want it.
