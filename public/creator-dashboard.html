🌐 Custom Domain
Point your own domain to your community page. Members visit markets.yourdomain.com instead of hyperflex.network/yourslug.
You control the URL — same page, your brand.
📋 DNS Setup Instructions
Add these two records in your DNS provider (Cloudflare, GoDaddy, etc.):
CNAME record
Name: markets.yourdomain.com
Value: hyperflex.network
TXT record (for verification)
Name: _hyperflex-verify.markets.yourdomain.com
Value: —
⏱ DNS changes can take up to 24 hours to propagate. SSL is handled automatically by Railway once the CNAME resolves.
