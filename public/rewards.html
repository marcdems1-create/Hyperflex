FAQ
Common questions about the rewards program
How does HYPERFLEX earn referral revenue?
When you click any Polymarket link on HYPERFLEX, our referral tag is automatically applied. Polymarket pays us 30% of the trading fee (~1% of volume) from referred users. We share a portion of this revenue back with you as USDC rewards.
When do I get paid?
Rewards are calculated weekly (Monday to Sunday) and distributed every Monday. USDC is sent directly to the Polymarket wallet address you've connected to your HYPERFLEX account.
Do I need a HYPERFLEX account?
Yes, you need to create a free HYPERFLEX account and connect your Polymarket wallet address so we can track your referral volume and send your USDC rewards.
How is my tier calculated?
Your tier is based on your total trading volume through HYPERFLEX referral links over the last 30 days. Tiers are recalculated daily. Trade more to unlock higher reward rates.
Is there a minimum payout?
The minimum payout is $1 USDC. If your weekly earnings are below $1, they roll over to the next week until the threshold is met.
