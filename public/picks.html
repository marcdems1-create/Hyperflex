How it works.

Three steps. No sportsbook connection needed.

01 · LOCK
Post your pick before tip-off.
Once the clock hits zero it's immutable. No edits, no deletions, no "I meant the other side." Each pick is server-signed with a cryptographic timestamp — stronger proof of timing than a screenshot, and distinct from a book-synced bet.
02 · GRADE
Official results settle every pick.
We pull final scores from ESPN within minutes of the buzzer. Units won, ROI, sample size — calculated automatically and tied to your profile. No one can quietly delete a loss.
03 · BUILD

Questions.

How is this different from Pikkit or Betstamp?
Those track bets privately. HYPERFLEX makes the record public. Bettors discover tipsters through the leaderboard, follow them, and — when subscription monetization ships — subscribe.
How does the timestamp work?
When you post a pick, the server HMAC-signs a hash of (your id, game, side, line, odds, units, the exact moment) with a secret that lives server-side. The database refuses any pick where the timestamp isn't before the game's tip-off. Anyone can hit /api/picks/:id/verify and confirm the row is original and pre-game. No edits, no deletions, no spoofing.
Do I need to connect a sportsbook?
No. Picks are locked with a server-signed timestamp before tip-off — a stronger proof of timing than a screenshot, and distinct from a book-synced bet. Book integration comes later as a separate "verified bettor" tier.
Is this legal?
Yes. We track picks and reputations. We don't take bets, facilitate wagering, or handle funds.
What does it cost me?
Nothing. Founding tipsters are free forever. When subscription monetization launches, you keep 100% of your subscription revenue for the first 6 months, then the standard 70/30 split.
When does monetization launch?
Q3 2026, after we've hit critical mass with bettors. Your record compounds value in the meantime.
