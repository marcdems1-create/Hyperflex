What is CLV?

Closing Line Value. The cents you beat the market's closing price by.

Bought a YES share at 30¢, market closed at 45¢? You captured +15¢ CLV. Beating the close is the strongest signal of sharp picks — it's what the market decided the right price was after everyone voted with their wallet.

Positive CLV over time = you're consistently getting better prices than the market thinks fair. Negative = you're paying up.
