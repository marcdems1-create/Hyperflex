How it works · in detail
01Polymarket runs the program. A subset of active markets gets a liquidity-rewards budget — typically a daily USDC pool paid to traders who post maker orders inside the program's spread (e.g. within 2¢ of mid). Markets in the list above are the ones with a budget right now.
02Post a limit, not a market order. Click a market, open the trade widget, switch to Limit. Set your price inside the spread shown on the card. Min-size matters — orders below the min-size for that market don't accrue. Sitting on the book is the work; the longer the order rests, the more you earn.
03Polymarket attributes + pays. Rewards accrue continuously and settle in USDC. HYPERFLEX runs the trade through its builder route so the order placement + attribution path is the same as polymarket.com. Polymarket's rewards docs ↗
01Sponsor funds a pool. A market maker, prop desk, or platform deposits a reward pool against a specific Polymarket market — e.g. $5,000 to seed depth on the YES side at 30¢ before resolution.
02Traders take the aligned side. Any user who places a YES position through the HYPERFLEX builder route earns a per-unit reward from the pool, on top of the prediction's normal payout. Reward rates are public and ratable per pool.
03Pool drains, market resolves. Pool pays out as positions are taken, capped at the pool size. Verified track record + builder-route attribution prove eligibility — no claim form. Funds settle on resolution.
