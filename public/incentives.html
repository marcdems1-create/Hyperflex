How it works
01Sponsor funds a pool. A market maker, prop desk, or platform deposits a reward pool against a specific Polymarket market — e.g. $5,000 to seed depth on the YES side at 30¢ before resolution.
02Traders take the aligned side. Any user who places a YES position through the HYPERFLEX builder route earns a per-unit reward from the pool, on top of the prediction's normal payout. Reward rates are public and ratable per pool.
03Pool drains, market resolves. Pool pays out as positions are taken, capped at the pool size. Verified track record + builder-route attribution prove eligibility — no claim form. Funds settle on resolution.
