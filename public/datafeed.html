FAQ

How do you define a "sharp" wallet?▾
Each wallet gets a 0–100 sharpness score recomputed nightly from realized P&L (40pt), take accuracy (30pt), trading volume (20pt), and position count (10pt). Wallets with score ≥ 60 count as "sharp" and are weighted proportionally in the sentiment score. Top-50 Polymarket whales get a floor based on their leaderboard rank.
What's the freshness? How often do snapshots update?▾
Snapshots roll hourly at HH:05 UTC, capturing the prior hour's reactions + comments. Wallet scores refresh nightly at 03:00 UTC. The contrarian feed filters to snapshots < 24h old. If you need sub-hour buckets, the Enterprise tier supports 1m and 5m windows.
Can I use this for redistribution or product integration?▾
Pro and Premium are licensed for internal use only. Enterprise includes a redistribution license if you want to embed sentiment into a product you sell to third parties. Reach out and we'll cut a data-licensing agreement.
What markets are covered?▾
Every Polymarket market that has received at least one user take or reaction on Hyperflex. The ~200 most active markets every week. Coverage expands as the user base grows — and since every reaction is tied to a verified wallet, we never generate ghost signal.
How do you handle back-fills and reprocessing?▾
The hourly snapshot cron is idempotent via UNIQUE(market_slug, condition_id, bucket_start) + ON CONFLICT DO UPDATE. If a snapshot was produced with stale sharp weights, the next run reconciles it. We don't delete historical snapshots — you see the full time-series.
