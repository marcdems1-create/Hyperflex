How scores work

Three different lenses on Polymarket traders. All data updated daily.

SHARPEST  → Calibration score (0-100). Derived from Brier scoring — how well-calibrated your confidence is across all resolved predictions.
MOST PROFITABLE  → Realized P&L. Actual USDC made or lost on closed Polymarket positions.
BIGGEST WHALES  → Total volume deployed. Tracks footprint, not accuracy. Capital ≠ skill.
