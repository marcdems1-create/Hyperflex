Whale Score vs. Flex Score

Two different questions about Polymarket traders. Both 0-100. Both updated daily. Different inputs, different uses.

WHALE SCORE  → capital-flow heuristic. Reads PnL + position size + leaderboard rank. Answers "how big is this account's footprint?"
FLEX SCORE  → verified-accuracy rating. Reads win rate + ROI on a log curve + calibration + consistency. Answers "how often is this trader right per dollar staked?"
📊Whale Score stays high while the wallet keeps deploying capital — it tracks footprint, not accuracy. Losing big still counts as big.
🎯Flex Score requires ≥5 settled events to surface. Below that, the wallet is "Building" — no public ranking until the sample is real.
🔗A wallet can be a top-rank Whale and a bottom-rank Flex at the same time. Capital ≠ skill. That's the whole point of splitting them.
