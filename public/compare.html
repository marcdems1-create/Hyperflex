Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free Polymarket analytics tool?
HYPERFLEX and Betmoar are both completely free with no feature gating. HYPERFLEX offers the most comprehensive free package with whale tracking, AI analysis (Crystal Ball), edge scoring, copy trading, and cross-platform portfolio tracking across Polymarket, Kalshi, and Manifold.
Which tool has the best whale tracking?
HYPERFLEX tracks the top 50 Polymarket whales in real-time with $160M+ in tracked positions. It also offers Telegram alerts for $10K+ whale moves, copy-bot trade mirroring, and an 8-signal Edge Score that factors in whale consensus. Betmoar and Polymarket Analytics also offer whale tracking but without the automated scoring layer.
Can I trade directly from these analytics tools?
HYPERFLEX and Betmoar both support built-in trade execution via the Polymarket CLOB. HYPERFLEX also offers copy-bot functionality to automatically mirror whale trades. Most other analytics tools (Polymarket Analytics, Polysights, Hashdive) are view-only and require you to switch to Polymarket to place trades.
Which tool supports multiple prediction market platforms?
HYPERFLEX supports the widest range: Polymarket, Kalshi, and Manifold in a unified portfolio view. Polymarket Analytics and Hashdive support Polymarket and Kalshi. Betmoar and Polysights currently support Polymarket only.
What is Edge Score and Sharp Score?
Edge Score is HYPERFLEX's proprietary market scoring system that combines 8 independent signals: whale consensus, volume, orderbook depth, capital flow, momentum, time decay, expiry, and price divergence. Sharp Score rates individual traders on a 0-100 scale based on win rate, trade volume, profitability, and cross-platform activity. These are unique to HYPERFLEX.
Is there a Polymarket portfolio tracker that works with Kalshi?
Yes. HYPERFLEX, Polymarket Analytics, and Hashdive all support cross-platform portfolio tracking with Kalshi. HYPERFLEX also includes Manifold Markets, making it the most comprehensive unified portfolio tracker available.
