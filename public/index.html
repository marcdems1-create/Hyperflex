How it works

From signup to live market in under 5 minutes.

01
🏗️

Create your community

Sign up, pick a URL slug, name your points currency (e.g. "Bucks", "Coins", "Credits"), and choose your brand color. Done in 60 seconds.

02
🤖

Generate markets with AI

Paste a YouTube or Twitch URL and the AI scans comments, chat, and transcript — then generates ready-to-publish prediction markets your audience already cares about.

03
📣

Share and watch it grow

Drop your community link in your bio, Discord, or newsletter. Your audience signs up, places predictions, climbs the leaderboard, and unlocks rewards you define.

Features

Everything your community needs to predict.

🤖

AI Market Generation

Scan any YouTube or Twitch URL and let the AI write the markets. Pull from comments, transcripts, or live chat — or paste Reddit, Discord, or any community text.

  • Scans YouTube comments, transcript & live chat — all at once
  • Paste Twitch chat, Reddit threads, or Discord messages
  • One URL → ready-to-publish markets in seconds
  • Validates question quality before publishing
  • AI resolution suggestions with source data
💎

Custom Flex Points

Name your community currency anything. Define reward tiers that unlock perks when fans hit point thresholds — Discord roles, merch, shout-outs.

  • Fully custom points name and branding
  • Live leaderboard with real-time balances
  • Creator-defined reward tiers with descriptions
  • Play-money only — no compliance risk
  • Members see locked/unlocked reward status
📊

Creator Analytics

Track what your community actually cares about. See trader counts, volume, top predictors, and a resolution queue with AI-powered outcome suggestions.

  • Total traders and weekly activity stats
  • Per-market volume and live odds
  • Top traders leaderboard with balances
  • Resolution queue with AI verdict assist
  • Pulsing live indicators on active markets
📈

Dynamic Live Odds

Markets move in real time. Every trade shifts the odds — just like real prediction markets — so members have skin in the game and reason to keep watching.

  • CPMM pricing — odds update after every trade
  • Yes/No price shown live on every market card
  • Early bettors rewarded for correct contrarian picks
  • More engaging than static polls — outcomes actually matter
  • Price history visible to members in real time
🔥

Streak Gamification

Keep members coming back with win streaks that multiply payouts. The longer the streak, the bigger the reward — turning casual predictors into daily habits.

  • 3-win streak → 1.5× payout multiplier
  • 5+ win streak → 2× payout multiplier
  • Streak badges on leaderboard (🔥 and ⚡ Streak Master)
  • Weekly top-predictor spotlight panel
  • Drives daily check-ins and retention
🔗

Built-in Referral System

Every member gets a personal invite link. When they bring someone new in, both parties earn Flex Points — a built-in growth loop that rewards your most engaged fans.

  • Unique personal invite links, auto-tracked per member
  • Both referrer and new member earn configurable rewards
  • Creator sets reward amounts and weekly cap
  • Auto-claimed on registration — zero friction
  • Turns your top fans into a growth channel
